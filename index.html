About Us

"As a team, we are driven by ambitious goals and have a visionary approach to address the challenges faced by our society. Our primary duty is to create innovative technological solutions for contemporary problems. In this regard, we have developed a startup called Rootbus, which aims to assist individuals who face difficulties in accessing public transportation.

Our vision is to provide seamless mobility technology that enables everyone to easily navigate their journeys. Through our extensive research, we have discovered that a significant number of individuals, approximately 10 out of 1, are unaware of which bus will take them to their desired destination. Therefore, our startup aims to provide a solution to this modern problem. We are eager to join forces with you and offer our services to enhance your transportation experience as soon as possible."
